About St. Mary Convent School

Founded in 1995, St. Mary Convent School has grown into a trusted institution offering high-quality education. We are dedicated to nurturing each child’s intellectual, social, and emotional development.

History

Our school started with 100 students and 5 teachers and has now grown to a full-fledged campus with over 1000 students and excellent faculty. Over the years, the school has developed modern classrooms, laboratories, libraries, and sports facilities to support quality education.

Mission & Vision

Vision: To empower students with knowledge, values, and confidence to excel in life.

Mission: Providing a safe, stimulating, and engaging learning environment for holistic development.

Achievements

  • Top ranking in state academic exams for 5 consecutive years
  • Outstanding performance in sports and cultural competitions
  • ISO certified educational institution
  • Strong alumni network contributing globally

Faculty & Activities

Our experienced teachers employ modern teaching methods, interactive learning, and mentorship programs to support every student. We conduct regular workshops, seminars, and field trips to create an engaging learning environment.

Students actively participate in sports, debates, science exhibitions, cultural programs, and leadership activities that help them build confidence and creativity.
