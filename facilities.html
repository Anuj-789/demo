Our Facilities

St. Mary Convent School offers modern and well-equipped facilities to ensure the best learning environment for students.

Smart Classrooms

Interactive smart boards, digital content, and multimedia teaching aids for better learning.

Computer Lab

High-speed computers with internet access for coding, research, and IT education.

Library

Extensive collection of books, e-resources, and a quiet reading environment.

Science Lab

Fully equipped physics, chemistry, and biology labs for hands-on learning.

Playground

Spacious playground for sports like football, cricket, basketball, and athletics.

Transport Facility

Safe and reliable school buses covering nearby areas for student convenience.
