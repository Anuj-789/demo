Admissions Open 2025-26

Admissions are open for classes Nursery to Grade 12. Age criteria and prior qualifications may apply.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Report (if applicable)
  • Passport Size Photographs of the student and parent
  • Address Proof
